Data Collection None

TabCutter does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers

Camera Usage

TabCutter uses your device's camera solely to scan receipts. Photos are processed entirely on your device using Apple's built-in Vision framework. Receipt images are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or transmitted to any server.

On-Device Processing

All text recognition (OCR) and data processing happens locally on your iPhone. TabCutter uses:

  • Apple Vision Framework ‚Äì for on-device text recognition

Data Storage

All receipt data (scanned images, item names, prices, and split information) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • Deleted when you remove the app
  • Not backed up to any external service by us

Third-Party Services

TabCutter does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. We use zero third-party SDKs.

Children's Privacy

TabCutter does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since the app does not collect any data at all, it is safe for users of all ages.

Sharing Feature

TabCutter includes a share function that generates a text summary of your bill split. When you choose to share, your device's standard share sheet is used. We have no control over and no access to what you share or with whom.
